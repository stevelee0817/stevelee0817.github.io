About Me

Engineering with broader judgment.

I am a Computer Science and Engineering student at Seoul National University. My technical interests include algorithms, web development, artificial intelligence, digital systems, and the design of reliable software.

I also work across questions that sit outside a single department: how technology should be governed, how incentives shape digital platforms, how ethical reasoning changes product decisions, and how education systems help people learn difficult ideas. This PPE-style perspective helps me connect implementation details with institutional, human, and social consequences.

  • Mentor for freshman programs and university student activities
  • Active member of student communities and academic, career, and interdisciplinary programs
